# Daily Command Center

## Sprint Goal

Sign one new school client by June 30, 2026.

Primary daily constraint: 2 focused hours.

Market protection rule: do not contact Boulder, Longmont, Broomfield, Erie, Lafayette, Louisville, Superior, or Gunbarrel prospects unless you intentionally change the no-compete rule.

Default daily schedule:

- 20 minutes: add or clean prospects.
- 35 minutes: write and send personalized first-touch emails.
- 35 minutes: call same-day prospects and prior follow-ups.
- 20 minutes: create or polish 3-point audits for warm prospects.
- 10 minutes: update tracker and scorecard.

## June Timeline

### June 4-7: Build the first 50

Outcome by end of day June 7:

- 50 Montessori-first prospects in the tracker.
- Each has school name, city, website, phone if public, decision maker if public, enrollment signal, and first observation.
- Sending domain setup is verified before any cold email is sent.
- Boulder-area prospects are excluded from the live tracker.

Daily action:

- Add 12-15 prospects.
- Mark each as A, B, or C fit.
- Draft the first 10 personalized first emails but do not send until domain checks pass.

### June 8-14: First-touch outreach

Outcome by end of day June 14:

- 60-80 first-touch emails sent.
- At least 40 same-day call attempts.
- Reply rate measured.

Daily action:

- Send 10 personalized emails per weekday.
- Call 5-20 minutes after sending when possible.
- Leave voicemail only when it is likely to reach the director or admissions contact.
- Move prospects to Contacted, Replied, Audit Requested, Call Booked, or Nurture.

Adjustment rule:

If reply rate is below 3% by June 14, rewrite the first email so the first line is a sharper school-specific observation.

### June 15-21: Follow-up and second calls

Outcome by end of day June 21:

- 60+ total follow-up calls.
- 8-12 replies, audit requests, or meaningful conversations.

Daily action:

- Send follow-up emails to anyone contacted 2-3 business days earlier.
- Make second call attempts to A-fit prospects.
- Create 3-point audits for anyone who replied, answered, opened repeatedly, or is a strong A-fit.

Adjustment rule:

If calls are not connecting by June 21, shift calls to:

- 7:45-8:30 AM local time.
- 11:30 AM-12:30 PM local time.
- 3:30-5:00 PM local time.

### June 22-27: Sales-call push

Outcome by end of day June 27:

- 3-5 strategy calls completed or booked.
- 1-3 proposals sent.

Daily action:

- Move all warm prospects toward a 10-15 minute call.
- Use the call to quantify open spots, tuition value, inquiry volume, tours, missed calls, and follow-up speed.
- Send proposal within 24 hours of a qualified call.

### June 28-30: Close

Outcome by June 30:

- One signed 90-day Fall Enrollment Marketing Sprint.

Daily action:

- Follow up on every proposal.
- Use the timing angle: starting before July gives time to improve the enrollment path while families are still deciding for fall.
- Offer $1,500/month first-90-days fallback only for a strong fit where price is the clear blocker.

## Daily Minimums

- 10 personalized emails.
- 10 call attempts.
- 5 new researched prospects.
- 1 warm-audit draft or improvement.
- Tracker and scorecard updated before stopping.

## Stop Doing

- Rewriting the whole offer.
- Building more website pages before outreach.
- Waiting for perfect proof.
- Sending generic "marketing agency" emails.
- Critiquing schools harshly. The audit should feel helpful, specific, and respectful.
