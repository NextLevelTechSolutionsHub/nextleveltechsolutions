# Outreach Scripts

## Positioning Line

I help Montessori schools improve the path from parent search to inquiry, tour request, and enrollment before fall.

## Proof Line

Use this only when needed:

We are currently working with a Montessori school to improve their website, local visibility, and enrollment flow.

Do not say:

- We increased enrollments.
- We improved tours by a percentage.
- We have proven results.
- We guarantee new students.

## First Email

Subject: Fall enrollment for [School Name]

Hi [First Name],

Since families are making school decisions for fall, I wanted to reach out.

I help Montessori schools improve the path from parent search to inquiry, tour request, and enrollment.

I noticed [specific observation about their website, Google profile, tour path, waitlist language, reviews, or competitor gap].

Want me to send over the 3 fixes I would focus on first?

Best,
[Your Name]  
School Enrollment Partners

If this is not relevant, reply "no" and I will close the loop.

## Stronger Observation Examples

- Your admissions page mentions tours, but the next step is not obvious from the homepage.
- You have strong parent trust signals, but they are not being used near the tour request step.
- Your school appears to have a strong program, but the Google listing and website are not working together to make the tour path clear.
- I saw fall enrollment language, but the page does not quickly show what age groups have availability.
- The school has a great story, but parents have to work harder than they should to find the next enrollment step.
