# Sending Domain Warmup Checklist

Use this before sending from the new cold outreach domain.

## Required Setup

- SPF configured for the mail provider.
- DKIM configured and passing.
- DMARC configured at minimum with monitoring policy.
- Mailbox profile filled out with real sender name, signature, and reply-to.
- No link shorteners.
- No attachments in first-touch emails.
- No image-heavy HTML.
- No calendar link in first-touch emails.
- Plain text only for first-touch email.
- Include simple opt-out line.

## Sending Rules

Week 1:

- 5 cold emails per inbox per day.
- Manual personalization only.
- Call after sending.

Week 2:

- Increase to 10 per day only if there are no deliverability issues.
- Keep replies as the main conversion signal.

Stop or slow down if:

- Emails bounce.
- Messages land in spam during testing.
- Reply rate is near zero after meaningful personalization.
- Prospects mention spam or irrelevant outreach.

## First-Touch Compliance Checklist

Before sending, confirm:

- The sender identity is accurate.
- The subject line is not deceptive.
- The message includes a valid way to opt out.
- The message does not make unsupported claims.
- The prospect is a reasonable business fit.
- The email includes one specific observation.

## Daily Deliverability Log

Track:

- Emails sent.
- Bounces.
- Replies.
- Spam concerns.
- Positive replies.
- Negative replies.
- Calls made after sends.
