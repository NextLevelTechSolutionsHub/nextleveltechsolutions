# June Client Acquisition Sprint

Purpose: sign one new school client by June 30, 2026 for the Fall Enrollment Marketing Sprint.

Use these files in order:

1. `daily_command_center.md` - what to do each day in the 2-hour sprint.
2. `prospect_tracker.csv` - the single live prospect file, cleaned and merged.
3. `today_first_10_emails.md` - first non-Boulder prospects and draft emails.
4. `outreach_scripts.md` - email, follow-up, phone, voicemail, and reply scripts.
5. `fall_enrollment_audit_template.md` - one-page audit format to send to warm prospects.
6. `sales_call_and_close.md` - discovery questions, pricing logic, objections, and close.
7. `proposal_template.md` - simple proposal to send after a qualified sales call.
8. `daily_scorecard.csv` - track activity and decide when to adjust messaging.
9. `domain_warmup_checklist.md` - sending-domain setup and daily volume rules.
10. `excluded_boulder_area_prospects.csv` - removed prospects that may compete with the current client.

Rules for the sprint:

- Do not claim results from the first client yet.
- Do not ask the current client for referrals yet.
- Do not lead with websites, design, or AI.
- Do lead with parent inquiries, scheduled tours, waitlists, and fall enrollment timing.
- Every first email must include one specific observation about the school.
- Call after sending whenever possible. Email alone is not the strategy.
- Do not contact Boulder, Longmont, Broomfield, Erie, Lafayette, Louisville, Superior, or Gunbarrel prospects unless you intentionally change the no-compete rule.

Primary offer:

Free 3-Point Fall Enrollment Audit.

Paid offer:

Fall Enrollment Marketing Sprint, $2,000/month for 90 days. Use $1,500/month for the first 90 days only when the school is a strong fit and price is the true blocker.
