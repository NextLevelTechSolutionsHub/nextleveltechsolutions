# Wheelhouse Systems Business Master Document

Version: 1.0  
Prepared for: Wheelhouse Systems / WheelHouse LTD  
Primary market: Colorado and the Denver / Front Range commercial security market  
Purpose: This document is the central business reference for Wheelhouse positioning, service lines, sales messaging, website copy, offer structure, and future content creation.

## 1. Business Snapshot

Wheelhouse Systems is a Colorado-based security and technology integrator focused on commercial-grade protection, surveillance, access control, remote monitoring, jobsite security, school safety, communications, and smart building solutions. The company’s strongest position is not as a single-product installer, but as a practical systems expert that designs, installs, supports, and improves full security ecosystems.

The business combines hands-on field experience with modern security technologies such as AI video analytics, professional monitoring, cloud video storage, enterprise video management systems, smart access control, smart locks, solar-powered mobile surveillance, and sensor-based school safety tools.

Wheelhouse’s market advantage is the ability to translate complex security technology into reliable, cost-effective systems that fit the actual property, budget, risk level, and operational needs of the client.

## 4. Key Differentiators

### Commercial-Grade Design

Wheelhouse should emphasize that commercial properties are different from residential installations. Rear doors, loading docks, perimeter gates, outdoor lots, warehouses, school campuses, multi-site businesses, and job trailers require thoughtful coverage, reliable connectivity, and proper configuration.

### Integrated Systems Approach

Wheelhouse does not only install one device type. The company integrates cameras, access control, alarm systems, monitoring, cloud storage, video analytics, door hardware, smart locks, sensors, network infrastructure, and communications.

### Practical Engineering Experience

Across the source material, Wheelhouse repeatedly claims 150+ years of combined engineering and security experience. This should be used as a major trust signal, especially on commercial, school, and jobsite pages.

### Professional Over DIY

Wheelhouse should draw a clear contrast between big-box DIY systems and professionally installed systems. DIY systems often depend on Wi-Fi, leave blind spots, lack verified monitoring, and are not built for commercial layouts. Wheelhouse systems can include hardwired and cellular backups, professional design, verified alarms, video integration, and ongoing service.

### AI and Analytics Without Overcomplication

Wheelhouse should position AI as a practical tool, not a buzzword. AI should be described as helping detect people, vehicles, objects, suspicious activity, license plates, faces, smoke, fire, false alarm patterns, and business traffic trends.

### Service and Support

The company should lean into ongoing maintenance, service agreements, system health checks, troubleshooting, warranty coordination, training, and system optimization.

## 10. Cloud Video Storage

### Core Offer

Wheelhouse provides cloud video storage solutions powered by Eagle Eye Networks to protect critical surveillance footage with secure, scalable, redundant storage.

### Why It Matters

Local recorders can fail, be stolen, lose power, be damaged by fire or flooding, or be tampered with by employees or intruders. Cloud storage protects evidence by keeping video accessible even when local hardware is compromised.

### Benefits

- Secure video storage
- Remote access from any device
- Scalable storage across locations
- Centralized dashboard
- Motion and tamper alerts
- Secure sharing with law enforcement
- Encrypted transmission and storage
- Redundant cloud infrastructure
- Lower upfront hardware costs in some deployments
- Hybrid redundancy with on-site NVR or DVR systems

### Hybrid Positioning

Wheelhouse should not position cloud as a total replacement for all local recording. The strongest message is hybrid redundancy: on-site NVR or DVR systems provide high-resolution local playback and independence from internet speed, while cloud storage preserves footage if the local system is damaged or stolen.

Source material references plans starting around $20 per camera per month. This should be used carefully as “starting around” language, not as a guaranteed universal price.

## 11. Remote Video Monitoring and Wheelhouse GuardA

### Core Offer

Wheelhouse GuardA is a tiered remote video monitoring and surveillance service designed to provide dynamic, proactive, and affordable property monitoring.

### Strategic Position

GuardA should be positioned as the bridge between passive cameras and expensive on-site guards. It gives businesses active surveillance, video verification, dispatch options, disaster response, and cloud redundancy depending on service level.

### GuardA Service Levels

#### 01 Base: Live Video Guard Monitoring

Remote guards watch the property and respond to unusual activity. This gives clients overnight and after-hours protection without requiring a full-time on-site guard.

#### 02 Base + Video Verification

Remote guards verify suspicious activity or disaster events and contact police as the accountable party when needed.

#### 03 Base + Video Verification and Dispatch

Remote guards verify trespassing or other suspicious activity, then dispatch security to secure the property. Once on site, the guard may call police based on the situation.

#### 04 Base + Video Verification and Disaster Response

Security is dispatched when disaster events leave the business vulnerable, such as fire or property damage where the site cannot be secured. Guards can remain until the business opens or the property is protected.

#### 05 On-Site Guard Service

Part-time or full-time on-site guard service for properties that need a physical presence during specific hours or around the clock.

#### 06 Base + Off-Site Cloud Storage

Adds video cloud redundancy so CCTV footage remains protected from physical damage or theft at the local site.

### Key Messaging

- Remote monitoring turns cameras into a proactive security service.
- Video verification reduces false alarms and improves response quality.
- GuardA is scalable, from basic monitoring to dispatch and disaster response.
- Businesses get a stronger level of protection without automatically paying for full-time on-site guards.

## 22. Sales Conversation Framework

### Discovery Questions

1. What are you trying to protect first: people, property, equipment, evidence, operations, or compliance?
2. What happened, or what are you trying to prevent from happening?
3. Where are the weak points: front door, back door, loading dock, parking lot, gates, rooftops, remote areas, internal rooms?
4. Do you need to know what happened after the fact, or do you need real-time response?
5. Who needs access to the system?
6. Do you need remote access from a phone or dashboard?
7. How long do you need to retain video footage?
8. What happens if internet goes down?
9. What happens if the recorder is stolen or damaged?
10. Do you need monitoring, dispatch, or only alerts?
11. Do you already have cameras, alarms, access control, or network infrastructure in place?
12. Are there compliance, privacy, or school safety requirements?
13. Is this a permanent facility or a temporary/mobile site?

### Recommended Sales Flow

1. Identify the risk.
2. Walk or map the property.
3. Review existing systems.
4. Separate must-have protection from nice-to-have features.
5. Design the system in layers.
6. Explain what each layer does.
7. Provide installation, monitoring, storage, and service options.
8. Close with the next step: assessment, proposal, site walk, or deployment plan.

## 29. Important Accuracy Notes

Some source content includes strong claims around crime reduction, break-in frequency, and system outcomes. These should be used with caution in public marketing unless the company can support them with current citations or internal substantiation.

Avoid absolute promises such as:

- “Stops all crime”
- “Guarantees police response”
- “Eliminates false alarms”
- “Prevents every break-in”

Prefer:

- “Helps reduce risk”
- “Improves response quality”
- “Can reduce false alarms”
- “Designed to deter, detect, and document incidents”
- “Helps preserve usable evidence”
